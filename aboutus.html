Our Story

Founded in 2009, RPT Battery Solutions began as a visionary startup with a simple yet powerful mission: to revolutionize energy storage and accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy.

What started as a small research team in Shenzhen has grown into a global leader in lithium battery technology, serving customers across six continents with cutting-edge energy storage solutions.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of the clean energy revolution, providing reliable, efficient, and innovative battery solutions that power homes, businesses, and industries worldwide.
