Products

Anern offers a comprehensive range of LiFePO4 lithium batteries including 12V, 24V, and 48V systems. Our batteries feature advanced BMS (Battery Management System) protection, long cycle life (6000+ cycles), and are designed for both residential and commercial solar applications.

Our solar panels are designed to last 25+ years with minimal degradation. They feature high-efficiency N-Type and P-Type cells with power outputs ranging from 430W to 580W. All panels come with a 25-year performance warranty and 12-year product warranty.

Installation

Installation time varies by system size and complexity. Residential systems (1-10kW) typically take 1-3 days, while commercial installations may take 1-2 weeks. Our certified technicians ensure quality installation with minimal disruption to your daily activities.

Anern serves over 200 countries worldwide. We work with certified local installers and provide comprehensive technical support, training, and installation guidelines to ensure consistent quality regardless of location.

Permit requirements vary by location and system type. Our team assists with permit applications and ensures all installations comply with local building codes and electrical standards. We handle the paperwork to streamline the process.

Warranty & Support

We offer comprehensive warranty coverage: 25-year performance warranty on solar panels, 10-year warranty on inverters, and 8+ year warranty on lithium batteries. All products include technical support and maintenance services.

Contact our 24/7 support team at +86-20-89269660 or cooperation@anern.com. Provide your system details and issue description. We'll arrange remote diagnostics first, followed by on-site service if needed within 24-48 hours.

Solar systems require minimal maintenance. We recommend annual inspections, periodic cleaning, and performance monitoring. Our maintenance packages include preventive services to ensure optimal system performance throughout its lifespan.
