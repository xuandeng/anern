Product Overview

The Ymir 5k Air-cooled Prefabricated Cabin Energy Storage Battery System represents cutting-edge technology in utility-scale energy storage solutions. This system is engineered for maximum efficiency, reliability, and safety in demanding industrial environments.

Built with advanced LiFePO4 battery technology, the Ymir 5k delivers exceptional performance with over 6000 charge cycles and industry-leading efficiency rates exceeding 95%. The innovative air-cooling system ensures optimal operating temperatures while maintaining low maintenance requirements.

The prefabricated cabin design allows for rapid deployment and easy scalability, making it the ideal choice for utility companies, industrial facilities, and renewable energy projects requiring reliable energy storage solutions.
