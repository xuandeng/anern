Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our furniture, delivery, and services.

What is your delivery area?

We offer free delivery within 50 miles of our Torrance store for orders over $500. Extended delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you offer assembly service?

Yes, we provide professional assembly service for all furniture pieces. Our team will set up your furniture exactly where you want it.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original condition. Custom orders may have different return terms.

Do you offer financing?

Yes, we partner with several financing companies to offer flexible payment options including 0% APR for qualified customers.
