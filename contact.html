Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What types of solar products does Anern offer?

Anern offers a comprehensive range of solar products including lithium batteries (12V, 24V, 48V), solar panels, hybrid and low-frequency inverters, energy storage systems, and complete off-grid and on-grid solar power systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.

Do you provide international shipping?

Yes, Anern ships to over 200 countries and regions worldwide. We have established a global network of distributors and partners to ensure efficient delivery and local support for our international customers.

What certifications do your products have?

Our products are certified to international standards including ISO 9001, CE, FCC, and various country-specific certifications. All products undergo rigorous testing and quality assurance processes to meet global safety and performance standards.

How long is the warranty period for Anern products?

Warranty periods vary by product: Lithium batteries typically come with 5-8 years warranty, solar panels with 25 years, and inverters with 2-5 years. We also provide comprehensive technical support and maintenance services.

Can you customize products for specific project requirements?

Absolutely! Anern offers OEM and ODM services. Our experienced R&D team can customize products according to your specific voltage, capacity, size, and performance requirements. Contact us to discuss your custom needs.
