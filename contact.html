Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our products, services, and policies.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with original tags. Items must be in their original condition for a full refund or exchange.

Do you offer personal styling services?

Yes! Our expert stylists are available for complimentary consultations both in-store and virtually. Book an appointment through our website or call any of our locations.

How do I find my correct size?

We provide detailed size guides for each product. You can also schedule a virtual fitting consultation or visit one of our stores for professional measurements.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We also offer flexible payment options through Klarna for qualifying purchases.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 25 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Please check our shipping policy for detailed information.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After this time, please contact our customer service team for assistance.
