The 6th Linqing · China Bearing Expo opens on April 20

Published on 2024-04-05 • Expo

We are thrilled to announce our participation in the 6th Linqing · China Bearing Expo, opening on April 20. We will present our latest stainless steel needle roller bearings and hybrid ceramic ball bearings.

Visitors can expect live demos covering lubrication strategies, seal selection, and bearing life optimization. Our application engineers will be available for one-on-one consultations.

Highlights

  • New product launches with hands-on sessions
  • Case studies from robotics and medical device customers
  • Exclusive show pricing and bulk discounts

We look forward to meeting partners and customers from around the world in Linqing. See you there!
