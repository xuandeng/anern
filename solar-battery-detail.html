Product Description

As part of the solar power system, solar lithium batteries play an important role in energy storage, emergency backup and environmental protection. Anern solar lithium battery features modular design with small size and light weight, making it ideal for residential and commercial applications.

The LCD display shows various parameters of battery operation, and the LED indicator switches colors according to the battery operation status for real-time monitoring of machine operation. The system supports multiple parallel machines with automatic address acquisition without manual operation.

Using new A-grade lithium battery technology, our batteries offer a long service life of over 8 years. The advanced BMS (Battery Management System) provides comprehensive protection with 200A continuous overcurrent capacity, making them mainly suitable for household solar energy solutions.
